# Investor Pitch Slides (AI Credit Card Intelligence)

## Slide 1: Title
- AI Credit Card Intelligence
- Personalized reward optimization for India

## Slide 2: Problem
- Users lose reward value due to wrong card selection.
- Estimated loss can approach INR 50,000 yearly for high spenders.

## Slide 3: Why Current Solutions Fail
- Manual comparison is hard and stale.
- Bank pages are fragmented and not normalized.

## Slide 4: Solution
- AI optimizer with deterministic + ML ranking.
- Automatic wallet analysis and top-card recommendations.

## Slide 5: Live Demo Flow
- Input monthly spend profile.
- See top 3 cards, savings, and confidence score.

## Slide 6: Market Size
- India credit-card users continue growing rapidly.
- Large reward leakage creates strong product pull.

## Slide 7: Technology Moat
- Reward intelligence engine.
- Continuous card learning from web + PDF signals.

## Slide 8: Product Modules
- Optimizer.
- Wallet advisor.
- Reward simulator.
- Insight engine.

## Slide 9: Dataset Advantage
- Structured reward dataset with normalized categories.
- Confidence scoring per card and recommendation.

## Slide 10: Business Model

## Slide 11: Growth Strategy

- High search demand around card comparisons.
