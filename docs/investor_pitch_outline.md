# Investor Pitch Slide Outline

## Slide 1: Problem

Users choose suboptimal credit cards and lose yearly rewards.
Estimated reward leakage: INR 10,000 to INR 40,000 per user per year.

## Slide 2: Solution

AI credit card optimizer recommends the best card from real spending patterns.
Output includes expected monthly reward, yearly reward, and confidence score.

## Slide 3: Product Demo

Show dashboard screenshot with:
- Spend input
- AI recommendation
- Comparison leaderboard
- Savings and optimization KPIs

## Slide 4: Market Size

- India credit card users: 90 million+
- Average annual fee: INR 1,500
- TAM estimate: INR 13,500 crore

## Slide 5: Business Model

- Affiliate commission on card issuance
- Premium subscription for advanced analytics
- API SaaS for partners and fintech apps

## Slide 6: Technology Moat

- Reward optimization engine
- Growing proprietary card dataset
- Personalization path with ML scoring

## Slide 7: Roadmap

- Wallet integrations
- Bank integrations
- Real-time spending insights
- Continuous model improvement
