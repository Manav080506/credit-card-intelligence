# Monetization Strategy

## Freemium

- Free: basic optimization and one-card recommendation.
- Paid (INR 199/month): advanced insights, forecast graphs, compare workflows.

## Revenue Streams

- Affiliate commissions: INR 2000 to INR 6000 per approved card.
- B2B API: from INR 50k/month for fintech integrations.
- White-label dashboard licensing: INR 3 lakh to INR 10 lakh per client.

## KPI Priorities

- Recommendation conversion rate.
- Paid upgrade conversion.
- Revenue per active user.
- Partner payout realization rate.
